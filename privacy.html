Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

GravPack ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you use GravPack:

  • Account information — name and email address when you sign up or join the waitlist
  • Inventory data — items, quantities, expiration dates, photos, and locations you add to the app
  • Household data — household size, members, pets, and readiness goals you configure
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, features used, and session information
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the GravPack app and services
  • To send you expiration alerts, readiness updates, and app notifications you've enabled
  • To communicate with you about your account, beta access, and product updates
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the product experience
  • To respond to your questions and support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your inventory and household data is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

During the beta period, data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We will communicate any changes to data storage practices before they take effect.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, email) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Export your inventory data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the waitlist form.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our site.

Children's Privacy

GravPack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of GravPack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, reach out directly.
